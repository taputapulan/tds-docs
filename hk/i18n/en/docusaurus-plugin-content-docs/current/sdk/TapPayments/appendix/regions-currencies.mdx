TapTap Payments supports 173 countries and regions worldwide and over 40 currencies. In countries or regions where local currency is not supported, or where developers have not set prices, users will be charged in US Dollars (USD). Additionally, most currencies are two-decimal currencies, such as USD 0.99, while others are zero-decimal currencies, such as JPY 130. Please refer to the following standards when setting prices:
